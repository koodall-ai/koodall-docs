## Welcome to Koodall's SDK Documentation

This documentation serves as a comprehensive guide for integrating and utilizing Koodall's SDKs for both Android and iOS platforms. 

### Getting Started

To begin your journey with our SDKs, please refer to the following guides:

- **[iOS SDK Guide](/ve-ios/quick_start_guide)**: Similar to the Android guide, this document outlines the process for integrating the Video Editor SDK into your iOS applications, ensuring you have all the necessary information to get started quickly.

- **[Android SDK Guide](/ve-android/quick_start_guide)**: This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to integrate the Video Editor SDK into your Android applications, including installation, setup, and usage.

### Why It Matters

Utilizing our SDKs will enhance your applications with powerful video editing capabilities, allowing you to deliver innovative features to your users. 

### How to Navigate

- Use the left sidebar to browse through the SDK documentation by platform.
- Click on the respective SDK guide to access detailed instructions and examples.

We are excited to see what you create with Koodall's SDKs!
